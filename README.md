training_data.txt is a preprocessed subset of WikiText-2, introduced by
Merity et al. in “Pointer Sentinel Mixture Models” (2016).

The dataset was truncated to 763,000 tokens and divided into 763 sequential
chunks of 1,000 tokens. The WikiText-derived text remains subject to its
original licensing terms. See [DATA_LICENSE.md](DATA_LICENSE.md) for attribution,
licensing information, and preprocessing details.
